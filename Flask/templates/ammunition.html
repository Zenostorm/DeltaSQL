Ammunition

Ammunition is what loads into magazines or directly into the internal mag of a weapon. Most weapons use cartridges except shotguns which accept shells, and the RPG-7 which is loaded with a PG-7V anti-tank round. Ammunition comes in many variants that have their strengths and drawbacks, the most common variants being FMJ tracer and armor piercing rounds.
