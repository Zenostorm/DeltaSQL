Chest Rigs

Chest rigs are worn to protect the torso, some rigs also protect your arms. Your torso is the largest target on your body, so bringing a chest rig is essential for survival in the wasteland. Some chest rigs provide extra storage, it's advised to keep items that you need quick access to in your rig such as mags, ammo, or heals. Chest rigs mitigate most of the damage you'll take in a gunfight, but they are often the heaviest part of your kit.
